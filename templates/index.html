Detectable Diseases

High Severity

Bacterial Leaf Blight

Xanthomonas oryzae pv. oryzae

Water-soaked lesions on leaves that turn brown and dry. Can cause wilting and death of the plant in severe cases.

Medium Severity

Brown Spot

Cochliobolus miyabeanus

Small oval to elliptical brown spots with yellow halo. Spots can coalesce causing leaves to wither and die.

Low Severity

Leaf Smut

Entyloma oryzae

Small, round, reddish-brown spots that turn black and produce powdery spores. Affects grain quality.
